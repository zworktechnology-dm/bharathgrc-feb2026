How We Worked This Month:

While our standard approach includes structured monthly planning with posters and project showcases, this month we explored a more trend-oriented strategy by integrating entertaining and relatable formats alongside GRC awareness content.

The new pattern worked very well — generating strong views, comments, saves, and follower growth beyond our initial targets. The audience responded particularly well to the meme format and transformation-style content. On YouTube, a single video reached over 25,000 views, introducing Bharath GRC to a significantly wider audience in a single day.
💡 Key Insight: GRC is a niche product — consistent awareness content builds the trust that leads to enquiries. The saves (42 on Instagram) and multi-city lead diversity this month confirm that the content is reaching decision-makers and architects beyond the local market.

📊 Overall Performance Analysis

YouTube delivered the month's headline number — the "Technology Won. Art Lost." video reached 25,007 views with 333 likes and 17 shares in a single day, proving that trend-blended GRC awareness content can achieve mass reach. Combined with "One Client. One Chance." (3,033 views, 88 likes, +5 subs), YouTube generated 37,350 total views and 15 new subscribers this month.

Instagram reached 60,103 total views with the GRC showcase reel "Tag someone who loves home design" leading with 9,072 views and 413 likes — the highest like count on the platform this month. The "Nobody believed in me" brand story post generated the strongest save count (24 saves) and 22 shares, reflecting deep audience resonance with authentic storytelling. 20 new followers were gained organically.

Facebook achieved 47 new followers — the highest follower gain across all platforms — driven by consistent posting and content reach extending through the existing community. This makes Facebook the strongest follower growth channel for Bharath GRC this month.
